About

We are Troway Dirt. A Private waste management company Dedicated to Environmental protection

  • September 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our Idea was conceived while brainstorming on a social enterprise we can embark on to improve our scoiety. Th environment is all we have! We could not allow it to keep degrading by the day.

  • January 2020

    An Agency is Born

    Officially established Towards the end of January 2020, since we have been actively tring to evacuate as much dirt as much as possible in the locations we serve

  • MISSION

    Our mission is to clean every area in Lagos one LGA at a time. We seek to actively fulfil the SDG goals Number 3 - Good health and well being, Number 6 - Clean water and sanitation, Number 11- Sustainable cities and communities

  • VISION

    Our vision is to become the most effective Waste management and disposal service in Nigeria
